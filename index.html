welcome to vanamati

Vasantrao Naik State Agricultural Extension Management Training Institute (VANAMATI) was established in 1992 with mandate of human resource development of the Department of Agriculture. This included updating the knowledge and imparting the training on advances in Agricultural Extension and building the capacity of Senior and Middle level functionaries of Agriculture Department. This is done by oganising training programmes, workshops etc. on various subjects related to Agricultural Extension and other areas of importance in Agricultural Extension Management...
